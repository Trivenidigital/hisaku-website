## Challenge

Triveni Express runs a beloved Indian restaurant and adjacent grocery
counter, but all orders came through phone calls during service hours. No
website, no way for regulars to place an order on their lunch break, and
the team was buried in call volume every evening. The owner wanted to
capture the obvious demand without paying 30% to a delivery aggregator.

## Approach

Designed and built a fast, branded site with a clean online ordering flow
— pickup and delivery, Indian-menu-aware categories, repeat-order
shortcuts, and a checkout that doesn't fight the user. Wired up payment,
printed order tickets to the kitchen, and added email receipts with a
one-tap reorder link. Layered on local SEO so "Triveni Express" plus
cuisine searches would surface the site, not a directory.

## Outcome

Six weeks from kickoff, Triveni Express shipped a full website with
online ordering. Online now accounts for ~30% of revenue, the phone
stopped ringing off the hook during service, and regulars tell friends
to order on the site because it "just works."
