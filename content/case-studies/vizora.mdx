## Challenge

Digital signage networks are stuck in the era of manual playlists and weekly
USB stick swaps. Vizora wanted a product that used AI to schedule content
intelligently across thousands of screens, optimize creative automatically,
and let non-technical operators run the whole thing from a single browser
tab. The bar was: ship something a small team can actually sell to enterprise
retail, not a demo.

## Approach

We partnered as design and engineering from day one. Defined the core
primitive (a "channel" that screens subscribe to) and built the product
around it. Designed the scheduler, analytics, and content editor as a
cohesive system rather than three separate apps. On the AI side, we wired
up generative content tools and a recommendation engine that learns from
impression data. Shipped to a staging environment weekly so the founding
team could dogfood in real stores.

## Outcome

Vizora launched as an AI-native digital signage SaaS. Operators schedule
campaigns in minutes instead of hours, creative variants get auto-generated
from a brand prompt, and the analytics layer tells them which screens
actually drove foot traffic. The product now runs across retail locations
and is expanding.
