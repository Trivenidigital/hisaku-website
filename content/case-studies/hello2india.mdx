## Challenge

Hello2India is a local Indian grocery store with a loyal in-person following
but no meaningful digital presence. Competitors ranked above them for
obvious searches like "indian grocery near me," their Google Business
Profile was incomplete, and they had zero reviews strategy. Walk-in traffic
was healthy but stagnant. The owner needed more feet through the door
without burning a marketing budget.

## Approach

Started with the basics everyone skips: claimed and fully populated the
Google Business Profile, fixed NAP consistency across 40+ citations, and
wrote location-specific on-site content targeting the queries their
customers actually run. Set up a review-request flow at checkout that made
asking for reviews feel natural, not pushy. Built out a small content
library around Indian grocery categories with local-intent keywords and
published weekly.

## Outcome

Within 8 months, Hello2India ranks #1 locally for 14 targeted searches,
organic foot traffic tripled, and the review count crossed 200 at 4.9
stars. The store became a go-to recommendation in local Indian community
groups without a single paid ad.
